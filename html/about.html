about us

Nestled in the heart of Vancouver, Sufficient Grounds is more than just a coffee shop. It's a community hub where you can connect, relax, and savour the art of expertly brewed coffee. From the moment you step inside, you'll be greeted by the warm aroma of freshly roasted beans and a welcoming atmosphere that makes you feel right at home.

our story

Luca Marino worked as a public defender and attorney and for the first 20 years of his career. The exceptionally long hours of preparing for trials, meeting with clients, and arguing in court were made easier by another of Luca's life passions, coffee, and lots of it! After working on a greusome, high-profile felony case, Luca decided it was time for a break from legal work. Luca took a year off and traveled to the world's top coffee destinations, Brazil, Ethiopia, and Sumatra. While meeting local growers and tasting the best beans in the world, Luca decided to open a coffee shop in his home city of Vancouver, Canada. Sufficient Grounds was opened in the spring of 2018, becoming a local favorite for espresso and coffee afficionados. In 2019, Luca and star SG barista James Cooper made their debut at the Canadian National Barista Championship. James ousted the 4 year returning champion, earning the first place trophy - and major bragging rights!

Our Coffee

Sufficient Grounds coffee shop found early success, becoming one of Vancouver's best artisan coffee shops. Then, the Covid 19 pandemic hit. Although the shop continued to do well, it became an intense struggle to order coffee from abroad. Many businesses had shut down during the pandemic and worldwide shipping issues meant lower quality coffee. It was during this debacle that Luca and SG decided to expand into importing and roasting their own coffee. Luca contacted the growers he had met while traveling and found them eager to work with new importers. Sufficient Grounds continues to work with many of the same local growers that Luca met on his coffee world tour. Luca and members of the Sufficient Grounds team continue to travel the world, sourcing new flavours directly from the farmers that grow them.
